Our Projects

Rehab and Renovation for Site Readiness (IP EDGE I) - Mandi Bahudin

This project involved the rehabilitation and renovation of the site to ensure it was ready for operational use. The work included structural repairs, electrical upgrades, and installation of necessary equipment to meet the standards required for IP EDGE operations.

Rehab and Renovation for Site Readiness (IP EDGE II) - Phalia

Similar to IP EDGE I, this project focused on preparing the site in Phalia for operational readiness. The team carried out extensive renovations, including civil works, electrical installations, and ensuring the site met all safety and operational standards.

Rehab and Renovation for Site Readiness (IP EDGE III) - Lohianwala and Peoples Colony

This project encompassed two locations, Lohianwala and Peoples Colony. The scope of work included comprehensive site rehabilitation, structural enhancements, and installation of advanced equipment to ensure both sites were fully operational and compliant with industry standards

Revamping of Different Sites (Civil and Electrical Works)

LThis project involved the revamping of multiple sites, focusing on both civil and electrical works. The team upgraded infrastructure, improved electrical systems, and ensured that all sites were equipped with the latest technology to enhance operational efficiency..

Earthing of MSAGs for Stability

The project aimed at improving the stability of MSAGs (Multi-Service Access Gateways) by implementing effective earthing solutions. This involved installing grounding systems to protect the equipment from electrical surges and ensure reliable performance..

PES GTR

This project focused on the PES GTR (Power Equipment and Systems General Technical Review). The team conducted a thorough review of the power equipment and systems, identifying areas for improvement and implementing necessary upgrades to enhance performance and reliability.

Pilot Project COM with Different Regional Stations (NE & NTDC)

This pilot project involved collaboration with different regional stations, including NE (North East) and NTDC (National Transmission and Dispatch Company). The objective was to test and implement new communication technologies to improve connectivity and operational efficiency across the regions.

OLT Site Readiness Phase I

The project aimed at preparing OLT (Optical Line Terminal) sites for operational readiness. The team carried out site assessments, installed necessary equipment, and ensured that all sites met the required standards for efficient operation

Three Year R&M of IT Equipment Project for GEPCO 2024

This long-term project involved the repair and maintenance (R&M) of IT equipment for GEPCO (Gujranwala Electric Power Company) over three years. The team provided ongoing support, conducted regular maintenance, and ensured that all IT equipment was functioning optimally.

R&M of Different DG Works in GTR 2021-22

This project focused on the repair and maintenance of different DG (Diesel Generator) works in GTR (General Technical Review) for the years 2021-22. The team performed necessary repairs, conducted maintenance checks, and ensured the generators were operating efficiently.

Maintaining Temperature Conditions for Equipment (Installation of ACs in GTR Phase I 2022)

The project involved installing air conditioning systems in GTR to maintain optimal temperature conditions for equipment. This phase focused on ensuring that all critical equipment was kept at the right temperature to prevent overheating and ensure reliable performance..

Maintaining Temperature Conditions for Equipment (Installation of ACs in GTR Phase II 2023)

Continuing from Phase I, this phase involved further installations of air conditioning systems to cover additional areas in GTR. The objective was to enhance the cooling infrastructure and ensure all equipment operated within safe temperature ranges.

Maintaining Temperature Conditions for Equipment (Installation of ACs in GTR Phase III 2024)

The final phase of the project focused on completing the installation of air conditioning systems across all remaining areas in GTR. This ensured comprehensive temperature control for all equipment, enhancing overall operational efficiency and reliability.
